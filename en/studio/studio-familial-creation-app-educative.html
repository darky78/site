Studio

Build4Kids Family Studio

A French family studio creating premium educational games that children love and parents trust.

Our mission

Help children learn naturally, with simple and positive mechanics and a design that makes them want to play — all without ads that could disturb our children!

Our philosophy:
Simple interfaces designed for young children, intuitive rules that are easy to understand, and concepts we develop as a family with our own children in elementary and middle school. This lets us test usability and effectiveness immediately.

👨‍👩‍👧 Parents design and code the app
👦👧 Children test it, share feedback and suggest improvements
✨ Together, we build every new feature with passion and rigor.

Funny, educational apps designed as a family, for children.

Our values

Clarity & kindness
Simple, readable and encouraging experiences designed for young children.

Premium quality
Special attention to design, rhythm, details and finishing touches.

Designed as a family
Apps imagined by parents and tested with real children.

Quality over quantity
Few apps, but carefully maintained and improved over time.

Parents’ trust
Reassuring experiences without unnecessary distractions or aggressive mechanics.

Fewer apps, better designed

We choose to develop only a few apps so we can devote real attention and quality to each one.
Every project is crafted, maintained and improved over time with constant focus on usefulness, reliability and experience.

A consistent brand identity

Our apps share the same foundations:
readability, simplicity, gentle use and polished finishes.
This visual and functional consistency strengthens perceived quality and builds a clear, recognizable and reassuring brand.

Clear and professional presentation

Each app has a dedicated page designed to be immediately understandable: screenshots, video, clear description and easy access to downloads.
It is a cleaner way to present our work to families.
