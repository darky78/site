Privacy policy – CriCri Animals

Last updated: 7 février 2026

Publisher: Build4Kids Family Studio
Contact: info@build4kids.fr

Summary: the app works offline and does not collect, store or share any personal data.

This policy explains how Build4Kids (“we”) handles information when you use the CriCri Animals mobile app (the “App”).

1) Data collected

We do not collect any personal data through the App. The App does not offer account creation, does not request credentials, and does not send data to servers.

2) Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because the App does not collect any data.

3) Offline operation and Internet access

The App is designed to work entirely offline and does not need Internet access to be used.

Note: the platform (e.g. Google Play, Android) may collect certain technical information related to downloading, installing or running the store. Those processes are handled by those services and are subject to their own policies, independent from our App.

4) Advertising, in-app purchases and third-party services

  • Advertising: no
  • In-app purchases: no
  • Analytics / tracking: no
  • Crash reporting / diagnostic SDKs (e.g. Firebase, Sentry): no

If we ever add third-party services (ads, analytics, crash reports, etc.), we will update this policy before they are put in place.

5) Permissions

The App does not need sensitive permissions (such as location, contacts or microphone) to operate. If a future version requires a permission, it will be explained during the update and through the Android permission screen.

6) Children

CriCri Animals is an educational game for children (from age 4). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children — and more generally, the App does not collect any personal data.

7) Data voluntarily sent (support)

If you contact us by email (info@build4kids.fr), we receive your email address and message content only to answer your support request. We do not sell this information and do not use it for advertising. We keep it only for the time necessary to process your request.

8) Security and retention

As we do not collect personal data through the App, we do not store personal information on servers. We still recommend keeping your device and operating system up to date.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy. Any change will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

10) Contact

For any question: info@build4kids.fr

Reference URL: https://www.build4kids.fr/en/privacy.html
