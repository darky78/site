CriCri Animals

An animal quiz designed to learn through play: recognition, listening, vocabulary and fun facts.

Quick info
Age: 4–12 Mode: Quiz Audio Fun facts

CriCri Animals is an educational game for children aged 4+ designed to learn about animals, build vocabulary and discover animal names in 6 languages with accurate pronunciation.

Children hear a sound, look at the animals on screen and guess the correct one. They then hear the exact pronunciation of the name, encouraging language learning, listening skills and memorization.

Created by a family, for families.

An app created as a family

CriCri Animals is not an industrial product.
It is a family project developed with passion.

We improve the app thanks to feedback from parents and tests carried out by our children. We test, adjust and polish the experience like a studio, with the sensitivity of a family: rhythm, clarity and encouragement.

Because learning can be simple, natural and joyful.

🧒 Interface designed for young children

CriCri Animals is designed to be used even before a child can read.

✔ Large clear buttons that are easy to tap
✔ Intuitive navigation
✔ Audio instructions
✔ Interface adapted to little hands

Children can play independently, even at the beginning of learning to read. It is also ideal for strengthening the mother tongue before introducing foreign languages.
