Fun & educational

With CriCri Animals, learning becomes a real moment of joy. Children listen, observe, guess and discover animals while playing at their own pace and without pressure. The mini-games encourage curiosity, memory and vocabulary in a positive, simple and cheerful atmosphere.

Safe, no intrusive ads

No unnecessary distractions or aggressive prompts here. Children enjoy a calm space to play and learn with confidence, and parents know exactly what environment they are in. No advertising, no in-app purchases, no sign-up: CriCri Animals focuses on what matters with clarity and kindness.

Designed as a family

CriCri Animals was born from a project created by a family, for families. Parents design it, children test it, and every detail is refined to make the experience simpler, clearer and more natural. The result is a warm, caring app truly designed for children’s real needs.
